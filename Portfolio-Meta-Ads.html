Full-Funnel Meta Ads Strategy with Klaviyo Integration

Company Overview

Cherie Amour (CA) is a nationwide boudoir photography company with a unique business model. Unlike traditional studios with fixed locations, CA operates with a team of traveling photographers who serve clients across 55+ cities. To maintain a premium experience, the company rents suites in luxury hotels where the photoshoots take place.


Project Overview

This campaign was designed to reach highly segmented audiences with personalized ads relevant to key life events or stages, encouraging them to book a boudoir photoshoot. By leveraging a full-funnel approach—Top of Funnel (TOFU), Middle of Funnel (MOFU), and Bottom of Funnel (BOFU)—the strategy effectively nurtured leads from awareness to conversion. The integration of Klaviyo allowed for automated follow-ups and precise audience segmentation, ensuring a seamless booking journey.



Campaign Strategy

1. TOFU: Awareness & Initial Engagement

At the TOFU stage, I created audience segments based on life events such as engagements, anniversaries, birthdays, and motherhood. Each segment received tailored video ads designed to capture attention and spark interest in a boudoir photoshoot. The objective was to:

  • Drive engagement through video views (50% watched)
  • Encourage visits to the landing page
  • Capture initial lead form sign-ups

For example, in the case of campaigns targeting moms, the messaging and creative were carefully crafted to resonate with their journey, highlighting the importance of self-care and confidence through a boudoir experience.


TOFU ad samples.

2. MOFU: Remarketing for Lead Capture

Users who engaged with TOFU creatives but had not yet converted were retargeted with MOFU campaigns. Each ad group received different video creatives aligned with their TOFU engagement behavior. These campaigns focused on:

  • Encouraging sign-ups through instant forms or landing pages
  • Adding leads to the Klaviyo list "Meta ad subscribers MOFU" for nurturing sequences

TOFU ad samples.

3. BOFU: Conversion-Driven Remarketing

For the BOFU stage, I implemented conversion-focused campaigns targeting users from the "Meta ad subscribers MOFU" list. The goal was to drive bookings by directing potential clients to a landing page where they could schedule a session. The strategy included:

  • Using Meta’s Conversions API (CAPI) to track real-time bookings
  • Implementing offline conversion tracking as a secondary verification method

TOFU ad samples.

Upon booking, leads were moved to the "Converted (str)" Klaviyo list, where additional data was recorded:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Date Booked
  • Session Date
  • Session Type
  • Booking Value
  • Client Status
  • Weeks Until Session

This list was used to:

  • Exclude booked clients from future campaigns
  • Create a Lookalike Audience for prospecting
