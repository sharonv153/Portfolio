Google Ads Campaign for Niche Industry: Boudoir Photography


Ad Copy Strategy

For this campaign, I focused on exploration searches to generate interest among potential clients. Ad copy was tailored to different stages of the marketing funnel, ensuring messaging aligned with the user’s intent at each stage.


Responsive Search Ads

Responsive search ads were the primary ad type used, allowing dynamic combinations of headlines and descriptions to match user searches. To maintain structure and clarity, I utilized a pre-planned framework:

  • Keyword-Focused Messaging – Targeting specific search terms.
  • Brand Messaging – Reinforcing the studio’s unique value.
  • Differentiators – Highlighting competitive advantages.
Each ad featured multiple variations of headlines (30-character limit) and descriptions (90-character limit), organized strategically with pinning where necessary to control messaging flow.

Creatives and Assets

Each campaign was supported by tailored creatives designed to align with the intent of the audience segment.

  • Custom Landing Pages – Dedicated landing pages with sign-up forms were created exclusively for this campaign. This facilitated better tracking and conversion rate optimization by segmenting Google Ads traffic from other sources.
  • Ad Assets – I strategically used sitelinks, callouts, images, promotions, and call extensions to enhance engagement and drive conversions.

Klaviyo Integration

To optimize remarketing efforts, I integrated Klaviyo with Google Ads.

  • Custom Tags – Leads captured through campaign-specific forms were tagged for segmentation.
  • Exclusion Audiences – Converted users were excluded from further awareness campaigns to prevent ad fatigue.
  • Progressive Funnel Targeting – Users were dynamically moved to more conversion-focused campaigns based on their interactions.

TOFU ad samples.

Marketing Funnel Strategy

Each campaign was structured to guide users through the buying process, ensuring a seamless transition between awareness, consideration, and conversion.


1. Top of Funnel (TOFU) – Awareness Stage

Objective: Introduce potential clients to boudoir photography and generate interest.

Audience Segments:

  • Affinity Audiences – Beauty, self-care, special events, gift ideas, and photography enthusiasts.
  • Custom Affinity Audiences – Users engaging with boudoir photography blogs or related content.

Campaign Types:

  • Display Ads – Visually compelling ads on beauty & lifestyle websites.
  • Search Ads – Broad and phrase match targeting high-level boudoir-related keywords.

Example Keywords:

  • Boudoir blog
  • Sultry photography
  • Glamour photography
  • Gifts for grooms

Assets Used:

  • Sitelinks
  • Callouts
  • Images
  • Promotions

2. Middle of Funnel (MOFU) – Consideration Stage

Objective: Engage users actively researching photography services.

Audience Segments:

  • In-Market Audiences– Users searching for professional photography services.
  • Custom Intent Audiences– Users searching terms like "Boudoir photographer near me."
  • Website Retargeting– Visitors who browsed the site but did not book.

Campaign Types:

  • Search Ads– Targeting high-intent queries with service-related keywords.
  • Display Retargeting Ads– Re-engaging past visitors with personalized offers.

Example Keywords:

  • Boudoir photoshoot
  • Boudoir photography
  • Boudoir session
  • Maternity boudoir photography
  • Bridal gift for husband

Assets Used:

  • Sitelinks
  • Callouts
  • Images
  • Promotions
  • Location
  • Call
  • Lead Form

3. Bottom of Funnel (BOFU) – Conversion Stage

Objective: Convert high-intent prospects into booked sessions.

Audience Segments:

  • Remarketing Lists for Search Ads (RLSA) – Users who visited pricing or booking pages.
  • Customer Match– Past inquiries targeted with exclusive offers.
  • Lookalike Audiences– New users similar to previous clients.

Campaign Types:

  • Search Ads– High-intent keyword targeting with aggressive bidding.
  • Performance Max Ads– Automated placements optimized for conversions.
  • Sales Ads– Promoting last-minute discounts and special offers.

Example Keywords:

  • Boudoir photography pricing
  • Boudoir photographers near me
  • Cherie Amour Boudoir reviews
  • Boudoir shoot prices

Assets Used:

  • Sitelinks
  • Images
  • Promotions
  • Location
  • Call
  • Message

Final Thoughts

This structured Google Ads strategy ensured a seamless user journey from initial interest to final booking. By leveraging a mix of keyword strategies, creative assets, and remarketing techniques, the campaign was designed to maximize conversions while minimizing wasted ad spend. Future analysis will focus on refining audience segmentation and optimizing for cost-per-conversion efficiency.
