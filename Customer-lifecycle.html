Optimizing the High-Ticket Luxury Buyer Journey


The Challenge: The Communication Black Hole

Cherie Amour (CA), a luxury boudoir photography studio with a nationwide footprint in 55 cities, faced a significant operational hurdle: a complex 4-8 month buyer cycle with no centralized visibility into the customer experience. Upon joining, I identified that the lack of a mapped journey led to fragmented communication and high churn during the 45-day post-booking window.



First simple buyer process draft.


The Solution: Cross-Functional Journey Orchestration

I led a comprehensive initiative to transform CA’s "transactional" communications into a customer-centric lifecycle strategy.

  • Stakeholder Collaboration & CRM Audit: I interviewed department heads and the COO to build a bird's-eye view of the process. I then performed a rigorous CRM audit of all manual and automated touchpoints (Email, SMS, Ads) to identify gaps and redundancies.
  • Strategic Gap Filling (Churn Mitigation): Data showed the 45-day pre-shoot window as the highest churn risk. I engineered a "Nurture & Prep" workflow focused on educational content (lingerie guides, "what to expect" videos) and anxiety reduction to maintain client commitment.
  • Expansion Revenue & Behavioral Triggers: I designed a "First 15 Days Post-Booking" workflow to capitalize on peak client excitement, using automated incentives and "Behind the Scenes" content to solidify the booking. I also integrated product education flows prior to sales appointments to build excitement and increase potential order value.
  • Omnichannel Orchestration: I led an initiative to transform CA’s "transactional" communications into a customer-centric Lifecycle Strategy, auditing all existing manual and automated touchpoints (Email, SMS, Ads).
  • Retention & Reputation Management: To improve the brand’s digital footprint and organic growth, I implemented automated Post-Purchase flows designed to incentivize referrals and encourage clients to leave reviews. This ensured the buyer journey didn't end at the sale but continued into a loop of advocacy.

The Results: Data-Driven Operational Impact

  • 10% Reduction in Cancellations: Proactive mid-funnel communication directly mitigated attrition during the high-risk pre-shoot phase.
  • Full-Funnel Alignment: The journey map now dictates the Content Strategy (Blog/Social), ensuring messaging aligns with specific client pain points at each stage.
  • Operational Clarity: Provided leadership with their first unified view of the customer lifecycle, enabling more accurate lead-nurturing and resource allocation.
  • Enhanced Brand Equity: Established a systematic process for reputation management, leveraging post-purchase high-excitement moments to drive organic reviews and new customer referrals.
