Engineering a Full-Funnel Attribution & Data Pipeline


The Challenge: Bridging the System Communication Gap

As Cherie Amour Photography scaled to 55 cities nationwide, the implementation of Acuity as an online booking system created a critical data silo. While Stratus remained the central CRM and ESP hub, the two systems lacked native communication. This fragmentation threatened the accuracy of conversion tracking and prevented a unified view of the customer journey from ad-click to offline booking.



The Solution: Multi-Stage Data Architecture & Automated Orchestration

To solve this, I designed a custom-engineered pipeline that ensured data integrity across the entire marketing stack.

Custom Data Capture (Front-End Engineering):

  • Developed and deployed custom JavaScript to extract hidden tracking parameters (UTM, gclid, fbclid) during the web booking process.
  • Successfully mapped these variables through Acuity to ensure every lead was tied to a specific marketing source.

Automated Mid-Funnel Orchestration:

  • Leveraged the Stratus API via Zapier to act as a central routing hub.
  • Configured automated triggers that instantly distributed booking data to four endpoints: Stratus CRM (Profile creation), Google Sheets (Conversion database), Klaviyo (Nurture automation), and Advertising Platforms.

Closing the Attribution Loop:

  • Engineered an Offline Conversion (OCU) workflow to feed high-intent data back into Meta and Google.
  • To maximize efficiency, I implemented an n8n automation flow to pass data from Google Sheets to Meta/Google APIs, replacing manual uploads with a real-time, self-hosted solution.

The Business Impact: Technical Precision & Operational Efficiency

  • Ad Optimization & Budget Efficiency:Enabled "Exclude" logic for converted users, shifting budget away from existing clients and back toward high-potential prospects.
  • Operational Scalability:Eliminated manual data entry between systems, reducing human error and freeing up team resources for high-level strategy.
  • Future-Proof Infrastructure:Designed the system with a "self-hosted" mindset by transitioning from Zapier to n8n, significantly reducing long-term SaaS costs while increasing automation complexity.
